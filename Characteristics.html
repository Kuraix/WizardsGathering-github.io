Characteristics of a good stream.
----------------

These are the following we believe can make a good stream.

1. Entertainment: Every good stream needs to be entertaining for people to like.

2. Respect: Every good stream needs to respect their audience because they are the main factor to make your streams popular.

3. Honesty: Be honest about you and your content to your audience so people will not assume you are fake.

4. Act Natural: Don't force anything and be yourself when doing a stream to avoid doing any mistakes.

5. Be Consistent: Show consistency with your work, always be passionate about your streams and never give up when the views are low.
