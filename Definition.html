Definition of Streaming
----------------

What is Streaming?


Streaming is a form/method of watching videos or listening to audio content
without the need to download the media files before watching

What is Live-Streaming?





Live Streaming is a broadcast that allows you to watch or listen to media content in real time. It is different in those pre-recorded
streamed contents in terms of your capacity to control how you want to watch it; you can easily pause the pre-recorded content and still go back to where you left it. However, in livestreams, you can pause it but you cannot go back to where you left it, as it happens real time.

Popular Types of Content in Streaming

-Video Games

-Movies

-TV Shows

-Music Videos
