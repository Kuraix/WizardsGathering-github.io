Audience Analysis

Our group has done our research analysing the audience of different streaming categories using the Emphatize Stage
For each streaming category here is what we have noticed with their audience.


Video Games: For the audience of Gaming streams we have noticed that they watch these streams because
1. They want to get better at Gaming, and 2. They enjoy seeing a streamers reaction to a game they like playing.


TV Shows & Movies: TV shows and Movies are classic forms of entertainment,
the reason why people still enjoy streamed TV and movies is because people love a good story,
It provides good entertainment for people who want to relax.


Music Videos: Music is a good outlet for people who want to relax,
or to express their feelings by singing or dancing.
It is also a good way to make people happy since everyone likes listening to music.


Conclusion: From what we have gathered from the different audiences from different streaming categories,
we have gathered that people have different reasons for watching a certain type of streamed content
but there is 1 common reason to why people watch streams in general.
It provides good entertainment.
