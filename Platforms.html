Platforms used for Streaming

There are many platforms that are being used for streaming
Here are the popular platforms used for a specific streaming content.

Popular Platforms in Streaming


-Youtube: Youtube is popular video broadcasting website in 2005,
it is the most popular website used for pre-recorded content or streams.

-Netflix: Netflix is an American streaming service founded in 1997
It is the most popular streaming service when it comes to TV shows and Movies.



-Twitch: Twitch is an American streaming service that was launched in 2011
Twitch is the most popular platform for live streamed content.

-Spotify: Spotify is an Swedish based streaming service founded in 2006
it is the world's most popular platform when it comes to the streaming of music.
